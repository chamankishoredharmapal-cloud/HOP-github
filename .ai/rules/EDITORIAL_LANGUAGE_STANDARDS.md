# Editorial Language Standards — House of Padmavati

## Brand Voice

House of Padmavati speaks with the voice of a knowledgeable, refined curator — someone who understands craft, tradition, and beauty, and shares that knowledge with warmth and respect.

## Voice Characteristics

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Knowledgeable** | Deep understanding of textiles, weaving traditions, and craftsmanship |
| **Warm** | Approachable and inviting, never cold or elitist |
| **Descriptive** | Sensory language that helps the reader imagine texture, color, and drape |
| **Refined** | Sophisticated but not pretentious; clear and elegant |
| **Respectful** | Honoring the artisans and traditions behind each piece |

## Content Guidelines

### Product Descriptions

Structure:
1. **Opening**: Compelling one-liner that captures the essence
2. **Details**: Fabric, weave, technique, origin, craftsmanship
3. **Styling**: How to wear it, what occasions suit it
4. **Care**: How to preserve the garment

**Example:**
> A Banarasi saree in deep amber, handwoven with gold zari that catches the light like ancient temple flames. The body is pure Katan silk — soft, luminous, substantial — while the pallu carries a traditional buta pattern reimagined for the modern woman. Wear it to a wedding, a milestone celebration, or an evening when you want to feel unforgettable.

### Collection Descriptions

Structure:
1. **Theme**: What inspired this collection
2. **Craft**: The techniques and traditions featured
3. **Mood**: The feeling and aesthetic of the collection
4. **Curator's note**: A personal touch from the curator

### Journal Articles

Structure:
1. **Title**: Compelling, editorial, search-friendly
2. **Dek**: One-line summary that draws the reader in
3. **Body**: Narrative, informative, visually broken with subheadings
4. **Images**: High-quality editorial photography
5. **Related**: Links to relevant products or collections

## Naming Conventions

### Collections
- Evocative, thematic names: "Heirloom", "Monsoon Wedding", "The Ganga", "Vermilion"
- Avoid literal descriptions ("Red Silk Collection")

### Products
- Descriptive + numbered: "Banarasi Tissue Gold Zari Saree I"
- Include key attributes: fabric, weave, dominant color
- Consistent format across similar products

### URL Slugs
- kebab-case
- Descriptive but concise: `/collections/banarasi-silk`
- No dates or numbers in slugs (except product SKUs)

## Prohibited Language

| ❌ Avoid | ✅ Use Instead |
|----------|---------------|
| "Buy now" | "Add to your collection" |
| "Cheap", "Affordable" | "Investment piece", "Heirloom quality" |
| "Sale", "Discount" | "Curated selection", "Special edition" |
| "Click here" | [Natural link text] |
| "Limited time offer" | [Eliminate — luxury doesn't rush] |
| "The best" | "Exceptional", "Remarkable", "Distinctive" |
| "Amazing", "Incredible" | "Exquisite", "Refined", "Superb" |
| "Girl", "Lady" | "Woman" |
| "Ethnic wear" | "Heritage fashion", "Traditional craftsmanship" |

## Tone by Context

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Homepage hero | Aspirational, poetic | "Where every thread tells a story" |
| Product page | Informative, sensory | "Handwoven on traditional pit looms in Varanasi" |
| Cart/Checkout | Clear, reassuring | "Your order is in safe hands — each piece carefully packed" |
| Error/404 | Graceful, helpful | "This page has wandered off. Let us help you find something beautiful." |
| Email | Warm, personal | "Dear [Name], your saree is on its way from our atelier to your home." |
| Studio/Admin | Professional, direct | "Update inventory", "Confirm order" |

## Spelling & Grammar

- Use British English spelling (colour, fabric, centre, jewellery)
- But: use "saree" not "sari" (brand preference)
- Use "handwoven" (one word), "heirloom" (one word)
- Capitalize proper names: Banarasi, Kanchipuram, Paithani
- Capitalize collection names: "The Heritage Collection"
- Use the oxford comma for clarity
- Avoid exclamation marks in body copy (okay in transactional emails)
- Use em-dashes (—) for parenthetical thoughts
