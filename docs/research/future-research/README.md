# Future Research — Knowledge Gaps

This document tracks topics identified for future investigation. When a gap is filled, it is moved to the appropriate category directory and removed from this list.

## High Priority

| Topic | Why It Matters | Suggested Source Type |
|-------|----------------|----------------------|
| **Pricing psychology for handloom** | Understanding the psychological price thresholds and anchoring effects specific to Indian handloom sarees | Academic papers, market research |
| **Diaspora consumer behavior** | The Indian diaspora is a key audience segment — their relationship with sarees differs from India-based consumers | Surveys, interviews, cultural studies |
| **Sustainability perceptions** | How premium saree buyers perceive sustainability, and whether HOP's natural materials and small-batch production are a purchase driver | Consumer surveys, competitor analysis |
| **Colour psychology in South Indian textiles** | Does colour preference vary by region, occasion, or age group in the target market? | Academic research, market studies |
| **Gift-giving psychology specific to Indian weddings** | Understanding the emotional weight, budget, and decision process for wedding saree gifting | Qualitative interviews, ethnographic research |

## Medium Priority

| Topic | Why It Matters | Suggested Source Type |
|-------|----------------|----------------------|
| **Alternative pricing models** | Subscription, pre-order, made-to-order — do any of these models build loyalty or reduce risk for handloom buyers? | Industry case studies |
| **Mobile commerce in Indian fashion** | How do Indian women browse and buy fashion on mobile, especially high-consideration items? | UX research, analytics |
| **Voice search for handloom** | What are the voice search patterns for saree queries? Could inform conversational SEO | Google data, search analytics |
| **Weaver cooperative economics** | Understanding the financial structure of weaver cooperatives to ensure fair pricing and ethical sourcing | Field research, interviews |
| **Social media consumption of craft content** | How do audiences engage with handloom content on Instagram, YouTube, and emerging platforms? | Social media analytics, content audits |

## Low Priority

| Topic | Why It Matters | Suggested Source Type |
|-------|----------------|----------------------|
| **Augmented reality for saree try-on** | Is AR try-on a value-add for premium sarees or a gimmick? | Technology reviews, user testing |
| **Blockchain for provenance tracking** | Could blockchain verify the provenance of handwoven sarees, and does the target market care? | Industry analysis |
| **NFTs for digital fashion ownership** | Does digital ownership of saree designs hold appeal for the HOP audience? | Market research |
| **International shipping psychology** | How do international buyers perceive shipping costs and timelines for premium Indian products? | Customer surveys |
